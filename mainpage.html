CHARITY RUN 2024

Welcome to Charity Run 2024, an event dedicated to promoting health and community spirit while supporting important causes. Join us at the Saigon Exhibition and Convention Center (SECC) in Ho Chi Minh City on October 11, 2024.


The goal of this running activity: to encourage sportsmanship, enhance community spirit and especially raise funds to support charity projects in many fields such as: education, health care, disaster prevention, helping the lives of people to be better.
